Red

Red means to stop, or feel alarm

Orange

Orange is generally used to warn of road construction, so I'd say orange is a color that signals alertness

Yellow

Yellow also means alert, but a softer shade can suggest sunshine and brightness.

Green

Green suggests nature, and in traffic, is a signal to proceed.

Blue

I think blue mainly suggests water and sky. The various shades can evoke anything from midnight darkness to a day at the beach.

Purple

Purple suggests royalty.

White

White suggests a blank slate.

Gray

I think gray shouldn't evoke much more than a neutral background shade.

Black

I think the right combination of black and white can suggest simplicity.

Brown

Brown can be combined with other colors to evoke Earth tones.

Pink

Pink can be used to evoke feelings of fun.
